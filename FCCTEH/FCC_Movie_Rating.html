Inception

Inception is a science-fiction action thriller film written, produced, and directed by Christopher Nolan. The film stars Leonardo DiCaprio, with a supporting cast that includes Ken Watanabe, Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Marion Cotillard, Elliot Page, Cillian Murphy, Tom Hardy, Tom Berenger, Dileep Rao, Talulah Riley, and Michael Caine. The film was produced and distributed by Warner Bros. with co-production from Legendary Pictures and Syncopy Films. Inception was released in both conventional and IMAX theaters on July 15, 2010. It was released on Blu-ray and DVD on December 7, 2010.
